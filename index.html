Arjay Grecia

Work History.

Arjay is a designer and web developer with a proven working experience in digital and print industry. He specialize in WordPress for business, learning management system and can handle working an ecommerce platform Woocommerce and Shopify.


He started working in 2008 in a webmaster staffing company in Davao City as a graphic designer and a webmaster for WordPress websites. In 2013, he moved to Cebu City and worked in a publishing company, he started working as a book cover designer and later on, he managed to handle a web design team to build websites for authors. In 2016 to 2018, he worked as an in-house graphic web designer and IT administrator in a trading company based Riyadh, KSA. During his tenure, he designs branding & identity and managing the company website. His biggest challenge was to develop a company intranet and maintaining IT infrastructure such as windows server, networt switches and routers.


Arjay started his small local based t-shirt printing business Inkpossible Design and Prints in 2011. He is passionate about helping business grow with his knowledge in web development and continuously learning to strengthen and share his experience in web industry, ui/ux, graphics, online marketing and advertising.
