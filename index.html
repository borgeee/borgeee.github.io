Arjay Grecia

Work History.

Arjay is a graphic web designer with a proven working experience in digital and print industry. He Enjoys working with business websites, Shopify, and WooCommerce. Also, he creates branding and identity designs, and doing product photography for e-commerce stores. Arjay continuously learn new courses to strengthen his knowledge in graphics, web design and development industry, including online marketing and advertising.


He started working in 2008 at Eversun Software Philippines Corp. in Davao City as a graphic designer and a webmaster for WordPress websites. In 2013 at Tate Publishing in Cebu City, as a book cover designer and as web design team leader, creating and building author websites with WordPress. In order to gain more experience in his field, in 2016, he was able to work abroad in Riyadh, KSA at Kayes Trading Est., he is responsible for multiple design work such as branding and identity design, social media graphics, product packaging design and developing and maintaining WordPress website.


Before joining Tate Publishing, Arjay founded Inkpossible Design and Prints in 2011, a local based t-shirt printing business.
