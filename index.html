Arjay Grecia

A Web Designer's

Journey.

Arjay is freelance web designer with a proven background experience in digital and print industry. Enjoys working with business websites, Shopify, and WooCommerce. Also, he creates branding and identity designs, and doing product photography for ecommerce stores. Arjay continuously learning new courses to strengthen his knowledge in graphic and web design industry, including online marketing and advertising.


Worked at Eversun Software Philippines Corp. in Davao City as a graphic designer and a webmaster for WordPress websites, Tate Publishing in Cebu City as a web design team leader developing author websites with WordPress, and Kayes Trading Est. in Riyadh as an in-house graphic web designer, responsible for multiple design work such as branding and identity design including WordPress website, social media graphics and product design.


Before joining Tate Publishing, Arjay founded Inkpossible Design and Prints, a small t-shirt printing business.
