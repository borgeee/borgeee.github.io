Read My Full Bio

Coming from a background of computer engineering, with a big dream of landing a job in the fields of computer hardware, networking or electronics… that didn’t go as planned. 😀

In 2008, with unexpected turn of events. I taught myself to design while carrying a little knowledge in coding HTML and CSS. I landed my first job in a web staffing company and it was my window of opportunity to be a web designer. I managed to improve my skills in design and WordPress was introduced in building blog sites. While the platform is gaining it's popularity in building websites, I began developing WordPress themes by converting psd designs.

Moving from working in a company, being a freelancer, opening a t-shirt printing business, book cover designer and web designer, to working abroad as an in-house designer and IT support. I’ve handled multiple design task and IT jobs (which was my dream); but, my biggest accomplishment was developing an intranet, a project management and archiving system using WordPress platform.

With years of grit, a lot of uphill struggle and learning, I am now happily working as a web developer and specialise in creating website for small businesses and startups. As of today, I already created multiple business website and particularly a learning management system for educational website.

02 | Web Design

If you are decided to collaborate in conceptualising web design or a product sales page, below is the idea of how we get started.

  1. Research - Tell me about the project, it’s purpose, and other information related to the project to achieve your goal.
  2. Wireframing - We focus on visual exploration to come up with best solution by arranging content elements using blocks based on user needs.
  3. Visual design - We create a UI styleguide in order to keep design consistency. From colors, fonts, spacing, padding and the usabillity of elements (button, forms, etc.)
  4. Repeat the process if neccessary.

You can check my behance page for design samples.
